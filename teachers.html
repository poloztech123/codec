Twebaze Melissa

Piano and Dance teacher

Melissa has been a multidiscipline musician and dancer for the better part of her life. She holds a bachelor's of music degree in sacred music with her primary instrument being voice. Originally from the US, she has had the privilege of making and teaching music in various countries around the world, with her favorite being the one where she now resides--Uganda. She is passionate about seeing people fulfill their musical potential, as well as using the universal language of music to help individuals and communities grow, thrive, and come together.
