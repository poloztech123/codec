Community Programs

April & December

Student Recitals

The Core Student Recitals provide a biannual opportunity for our students in the training department who are 18 years and below to get together. In font of family, friends, staff, and interested community members, we showcase and celebrate what they’ve been learning through performances on their instrument of study.

Annualy

Live at the Core

Live @ The Core is a live music and dance event that provides positive entertainment to the community in which we’re situated, while also helping them get to know The Core better. It’s a chance for The Core to interact with the community at large and the community to interact with and enjoy The Core and all it has to offer.

Every Wednesday

Jam at the Core

This is a weekly opportunity for anyone in the area to come access instruments and play with other musicians in order to sharpen their skills, network, and enjoy coming together for the love of music. Musicians in attendance stand a chance to be selected to perform at a Live @ The Core event.

06:30 PM - 7:30 PM
